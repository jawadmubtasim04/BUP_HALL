Hall Facilities

Discover the benefits of living on campus.

Smart Living Scope

Stay closer to the library, faculty, and your peers. On-campus students often achieve higher grades due to easy access to academic resources.

Close to the Class

You're just minutes away from your classes, the library, dining halls, sports zones, and the gymnasium. Everything you need is within a short walk.

Leadership Development

Living in a hall community fosters involvement and helps you develop crucial leadership skills and a strong sense of responsibility.

Cost-Effective Living

Hall living is an affordable option that bundles your housing, utilities, and other services into one manageable cost, making budgeting simple.

Wireless Connectivity

Enjoy seamless Wi-Fi access throughout the hall, labs, and library. Stay connected for your academic and personal needs anytime, anywhere.

Safety & Security

Your safety is our top priority. We provide 24/7 security staff, CCTV surveillance, and secure access to ensure a safe living environment.

Admissions

Your guide to joining the BUP Hall community.

Apply for a seat

BUP HALL PORTAL

Admission Eligibility

  • Must be a regular student of BUP.
  • Minimum SGPA of 2.5 for readmission/existing students.
  • No history of accusation or conviction from the University or Government.

Hall Fees

A new student has to pay a one-time admission fee in the 1st semester, and a recurring semester fee thereafter. Payments are due at the beginning of each semester during academic registration.

Payment Procedure

Payments can be made at the Accounts section at DSC or through the student Hall portal using various mobile and online banking methods.

Seat Cancellation

Students can apply for seat cancellation during specific periods announced by the Hall Authority each semester.
